Country Origin of the food: Batangas


History of the food: Bulalo is native to the Southern Luzon region of the Philippines. Many references have evolved regarding its origin. One of it says that this type of dish originated from Batangas where you find the many versions of Bulalo.


Ingredients of the food:Beef Shank Beef Shank,Water,Onion,Garlic,Black Peppercorn,Bay Leaf,Yellow Corn,Potato,Cabbage,Onion Springs,MAGGI® Magic Sarap,Toasted Garlic


How to cook the food: Step1 Simmer beef in water until slightly tender. Skim off fat as needed. Step2 Add onion, garlic, peppercorn and bay leaf. Simmer until beef is very tender. Step3 180 Min Add corn and simmer for 10 minutes. Add potato, cabbage and spring onion. Season with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP®. Transfer into a serving bowl, top with toasted garlic and serve.
